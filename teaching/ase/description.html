Applied Statistics and Econometrics

Description

The aim of this course is to provide an introduction to the practice of econometrics. While both theoretical and practical aspects of econometrics are covered, emphasis will be on intuitive understanding. Concepts are always illustrated with real world applications and using real world data. Students will have a hands-on experience using STATA for statistics, graphics and data management.
