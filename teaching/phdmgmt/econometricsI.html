Description

The aim of this course is to provide an introduction to the practice of econometrics with a special emphasis on application in the field of management sciences. While both theoretical and practical aspects of econometrics will be covered, emphasis will be on intuitive understanding. Concepts will be illustrated with real world applications on real world data.

Textbook

The required textbook is

Wooldridge, Jeffrey M. Econometric analysis of cross section and panel data. MIT press, 2010.

Other useful references:

  • Cameron, A. Colin, and Pravin K. Trivedi. Microeconometrics using stata. Vol. 5. College Station, TX: Stata Press, 2009.
  • Cameron, A. Colin, and Pravin K. Trivedi. Microeconometrics: methods and applications. Cambridge university press, 2005

Computer Software

The software that will be used in this course is Stata. No prior knowledge of this software package is assumed. The software will be introduced in class.
